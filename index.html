Department of Economics, University of Cologne, Germany

Welcome!

I am an applied microeconomist working at the intersection of theory and experimental economics. My main fields are market design of the digital economy and contract theory.

I study reputation systems to understand and improve the functioning of digital markets. Broadly speaking, I want to deepen our understanding of how we rate and how we interpret other’s ratings, and what platforms can do to optimize the informativeness of feedback profiles.

I am also interested in the optimal provision of property rights to create the incentives e.g. for the provision of public goods or incentives for innovation.

Bridging these two research areas, I also study subjective performance appraisals in the field of personnel economics.
