Welcome!

I an an economist in marketing, working on topics related to digital marketing, digital economics, platform markets and market design.

Currently, I study reputation systems to understand and improve the functioning of digital platform markets. I want to deepen our understanding of how we rate and how we interpret otherâ€™s ratings, and what platforms can do to optimize the informativeness of feedback profiles.

I am also interested in subjective performance evaluations in the field of personnel economics.
